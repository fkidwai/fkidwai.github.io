Website Evalutations

Good Website

Nerdwallet

This is the webpage for nerdwallet. A company that helps their customer pick the right credit card for them. It features many different links and images with a green and white color scheme.

Crap Principles

  • Contrast
  • Alignment
  • Repetition
  • Proximity

This website uses green and white throughout its site to contrast the black text. All of the different sections are aligned in the center and are in close proximity with each other and all of these things bring the page together to make it look nice.

Bad Webstie

Yale's Art Page

This is the website for yale's school of art. It contains information for yale's school of art. It gives updates and ways to apply to the school.

Crap Principles

  • Contrast
  • Alignment
  • Repetition
  • Proximity

This website's content is contained in the left side of the page and not aligned. It contains many different colors and doesn't not repeat its color scheme.
