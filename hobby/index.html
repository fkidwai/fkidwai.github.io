Schedule

UFC fights happen through the year. They are not held to a specific season like other sports. It is very much like boxing where fighters choose when & whenever they want to fight and they can fight throughout the year. On average most UFC fighters fight 2-4 times per year. Even though fighters fight an average of 3 times per year, the UFC holds fights every week. Most weeks its small fights for unranked and lower ranked fighters called "Fight Night". Usually once a month they have title fights called UFC ###, some number.


Off-Season Schedule

  • Rest and recovery period, usually lasting several weeks to months. Just depends on how long the fighter wants to rest
  • Usually fighters train to keep their skills sharp. They train strength and conditioning to maintain strength train different techniques to add to their arsenal of weapons
  • Skill-specific training, such as boxing, wrestling, jiu jitsu, muy thai, kickboxing, etc
  • Fighters could just do recover work by doing cryotherapy or deep tissue massages
  • Fighters can go to other gyms and train with other fighters to learn from them and their team

In-Season Schedule

  • Regular practice sessions, usually several times per week
  • Team meetings to review game plans, analyze opponents, and discuss strategies
  • Warm-up routines and drills to prepare for games
  • The team watches film on the opponent to understand their fighting sytle
  • The team holds multiple sparring sessions to train for the actual fight
  • Physical therapy and recovery sessions to prevent and manage injuries
  • Individual training sessions to work on specific techniques and skills and areas of improvement

Where?

Professional UFC fights happen in an Octagon cage. Whree both opponents are locked into the cage with a referee

Amateur MMA fights can take place in any type of cage of varying size, but most occur in an octagon cage. In the early days they use to happen in a boxing ring due to the lack of octagons avaliable

Although fights can take place anywhere. All UFC fights and amateur fights take place in an indoor venue with a crowd of people in attendance
